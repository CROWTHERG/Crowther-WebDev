About Me

Hello! I’m Hammed Daniel Ajayi, a passionate web developer and graphics designer with over 2 years of hands-on experience. My journey began with a curiosity for how websites work, and quickly evolved into a drive to bring creative ideas to life through technology and design.

I specialize in building modern, responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My toolkit also includes React for dynamic user interfaces, Tailwind CSS for rapid styling, and Git for efficient version control and collaboration.

As a graphics designer, I blend visual creativity with technical skills to craft engaging layouts, logos, and digital assets that enhance user experience and brand identity. I believe that great design is about both aesthetics and functionality.

Whether it’s developing a new website from scratch, optimizing existing projects, or designing eye-catching graphics, I’m committed to delivering solutions that are both innovative and reliable. I love collaborating with clients and teams to turn ideas into reality, always striving to learn and grow with every project.

Let’s work together to create something exceptional!
